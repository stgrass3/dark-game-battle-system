DARK GAME 規則 / RULES

遊戲介紹 / Introduction

DARK GAME 是一個純想像力的卡牌對戰系統。不是比較誰的牌更強，而是用你的創意和描述來贏得辯論。

DARK GAME is a pure imagination-based card battle system. It's not about whose cards are stronger—it's about using your creativity and narration to win debates.

核心原則 / Core Principle:
沒有數值計算、沒有傷害公式、沒有自動判定。
No damage calculation, no formulas, no automatic resolution.

卡牌類型 / Card Types

每位玩家抽取6張卡牌 / Each player draws 6 cards:

  • 種族 / Race - 角色的起源 Your character's origin (例如: 賽亞人, 魔法師)
  • 武器 / Weapon - 裝備 Equipment (例如: 妖刀, 鎖鏈)
  • 能力 / Ability ×3 - 技能 Techniques (例如: 黑閃, 時間暫停)
  • 召喚物 / Entity - 夥伴 Companion (例如: 九尾, 烏爾媞婁)

戰鬥流程 / Battle Flow

1. 回合制 / Turn-based

玩家輪流描述自己的攻擊。輪到你時，你的面板會發光提示。

Players take turns describing attacks. When it's your turn, your panel glows.

2. 描述攻擊 / Describe Attack

用文字描述你的攻擊！可以提到你的卡牌來加成描述，但並非必須。

Describe your attack in text! You can reference your cards to enhance descriptions, but it's not required.

3. 結束回合 / End Turn

說完攻擊後，點擊"結束回合"讓對手行動。

After your attack, click "End Turn" to let your opponent act.

辯論系統 / Debate System

如果雙方對某個攻擊有爭議 / If both players disagree on an attack:

  1. 進攻方開始敘述攻擊 / Attacker describes their attack
  2. 防守方可以點擊"辯論"來挑戰 / Defender can click "Debate" to challenge
  3. 雙方各自論述 / Both argue their case
  4. 由進攻方決定結果 / Attacker decides the result:
    • 算我贏 (Counts) - 攻擊成功 / Attack succeeds
    • 不算 (Void) - 攻擊無效 / Attack is voided

被動效果 / Passive Effects

某些卡牌有被動關鍵字（如: 舔血、吸收、再生）。當觸發條件滿足時，系統會提示你描述被動效果。

Some cards have passive keywords (e.g., 舔血, 吸收, 再生). When triggered, the system prompts you to describe the passive effect.

勝利條件 / Victory Conditions

  • 投降 / Surrender - 點擊投降按鈕認輸 / Click surrender button
  • 死亡 / Death - 在敘述中宣佈自己的角色死亡 / Declare your character dead in narration
  • 自爆 / Self-Sacrifice - 敘述自爆攻擊 / Describe a self-destruct attack

小技巧 / Tips

  • 不要只說"我攻擊"，要描述動作、氣氛、角色的心態 / Don't just say "I attack"—describe the action, atmosphere, character's mindset
  • 利用卡牌名稱來啟發創意，但不要被限制 / Use card names to inspire creativity, but don't be limited by them
  • 辯論時保持尊重，贏了要優雅，輸了要大方 / Stay respectful during debates. Win with grace, lose with dignity

享受想像力的戰鬥吧！

Enjoy the battle of imagination!
